# NNNN — Short title

## Context

What's the situation that forced a decision? What constraints (technical,
product, UX, regulatory, time) are in play? Two or three sentences is
usually enough. Link to the milestone spec or issue if relevant.

## Decision

The choice, stated plainly. One or two sentences. Active voice — "We will
X" or "Clearview Savings uses X" — not "It was decided that X."

## Alternatives considered

The other paths that were genuinely on the table, each with a one-line
reason for rejection. If there was no real alternative, say so — that's
itself useful to record.

- **Alternative A** — why rejected
- **Alternative B** — why rejected

## Consequences

What changes as a result? Both the good (what this unlocks) and the costs
(what gets harder, what we're now committed to maintaining). Future-you
reads this section first when wondering whether to revisit the decision.
